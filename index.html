About Me

I am Gaurav Bhalchandra Dichwalkar. I have done my Bachelor of Computer Applications Degree in MES College in Goa. As for what I am doing currently, I am pursuing Master of Computer Applications Degree.

I chose web developing as a career because, I was fascinated by the idea of how our lives have become so easy since the dawn of internet. For, example, we can talk, discuss, share and do much more using internet. And the foundation of all this is web. I wanted to make and develop websites which were easy to use.
